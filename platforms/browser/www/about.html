About us

a few words about our car wash

Cleanmotor is a well established car-cleaning business offering you a great service to look after your car. Every team member has been fully trained in car-cleaning to an extremely high level. Every-time you visit us, you’ll be met by the same experienced team.

We are looking forward to welcoming you and your car to our car wash. If your car needs any of the drive-thru services then please just visit us when it’s convenient for you. We will be glad to provide you with high quality car cleaning services.
